## Operator checklist (honest, based on current reality)

**Duleite upozorneni:** Neexistuje formalni accept/reject/comment workflow. Operator ma k dispozici pouze `preview_stage` sloupec v LEADS a Google Sheets revision history. `preview_needs_review` je boolean quality signal, **ne** rozhodnuti.

| Stav | Co operator vidi | Co operator dela | Co operator NEDELA |
|------|------------------|------------------|--------------------|

| `BRIEF_READY` | Brief JSON je hotovy, webhook zatim nevolan. | Pripadne overi Script Properties (`ENABLE_WEBHOOK`, `WEBHOOK_URL`, `PREVIEW_WEBHOOK_SECRET`) pokud se lead dlouho neposouva. | Neposousi stav manualne na `GENERATING`. |
| `GENERATING` | Prechodny stav — webhook request in-flight. | Ceka jeden timer interval. Pokud zustane vic tiku → eskalace (mimo B-07 scope jak reagovat). | Nerucne neprepisuje. Nevolat webhook rucne. |
| `READY_FOR_REVIEW` | `preview_url` je vyplneny, `preview_needs_review` je true/false quality hint. | Otevre `preview_url`, prohledne obsah. | `FAILED` | Posledni pokus selhal, `preview_error` ukazuje proc. | Precte `preview_error`. Pokud data-fix → upravi vstupni data, necha pipeline retry na dalsim tiku (`FAILED` je v `eligibleStages`). - "Pilot" v kontextu B-07 znamena **pilot-prep** (test-data + checklist). Skutecny B-08 pilot runbook (execution plan, success metriky, rollback) je samostatny task.
