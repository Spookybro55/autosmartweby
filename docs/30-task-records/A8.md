## Trigger a Workflow

### BRIEF_READY — definice

Stav `preview_stage = 'BRIEF_READY'` znamena ze lead ma:
1. `preview_brief_json` — serializovany B-01 `PreviewBrief` (18 poli, vysledek `buildPreviewBrief_()`)
2. `preview_slug` — URL-safe slug (vysledek `buildSlug_(business_name, city)`, max 60 chars)
3. `preview_headline`, `preview_subheadline`, `preview_cta` — mirror z brief JSON
4. `template_type` — vysledek `chooseTemplateType_()` (viz B-03 family mapping)
5. Pokud `send_allowed === 'TRUE'`: `email_subject_draft` + `email_body_draft` + `outreach_stage = 'DRAFT_READY'`
6. `lead_stage: QUALIFIED -> IN_PIPELINE`
7. `last_processed_at` — timestamp

BRIEF_READY je terminal stav v DRY_RUN/no-webhook rezimu. V production (`ENABLE_WEBHOOK=true`, `WEBHOOK_URL` set) pokracuje na QUEUED -> SENT_TO_WEBHOOK -> READY/REVIEW_NEEDED.

### Trigger cesty do processPreviewQueue (2 paths)

1. **Time-based** (pre-existing): 15-min timer `processPreviewQueue` instalovany pres `installProjectTriggers()` (PreviewPipeline.gs:1372).
2. **Post-qualify hook** (A-08, new): po uspesne kvalifikaci v `runAutoQualify_()` (stats.qualified > 0, ne dry run) volano `processPreviewQueue()` inline. Non-fatal: chyba preview hooku nezneplatni qualify vysledek.

### Eligible row conditions v processPreviewQueue

- `qualified_for_preview === 'TRUE'`
- `preview_stage ∈ {'', 'not_started', 'failed', 'review_needed', 'brief_ready'}`
- `dedupe_flag !== 'TRUE'`
- Skip pokud `preview_stage === 'brief_ready'` AND (`DRY_RUN || !ENABLE_WEBHOOK || !WEBHOOK_URL`) — zabrani zbytecnemu rebuildu

### Fail handling per-row

Per-row `try/catch` uvnitr hlavni smycky (PreviewPipeline.gs:938-1065):
- Row failure → `preview_stage = FAILED`, `preview_error = 'PROCESSING_ERROR: ' + message`
- Batch pokracuje k dalsimu radku
- `writeExtensionColumns_()` na konci zapise vsechny zmeny batch-em
