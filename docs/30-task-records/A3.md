## Contracts Changed

Novy kontrakt: **Normalization raw to LEADS v1.0**.

- **Field mapping:** 23 poli celkem. 15 z `raw_payload_json`, 5 z `_raw_import` metadat, 3 generovane/konstantni.
- **Target column resolution:** 6 legacy hardcoded pozic (`LEGACY_COL` z `Config.gs:44`), 10 pres `HeaderResolver`, 6 novych extension sloupcu append-only.
- **Cleaning:** vsechny operace pres existujici helpery (`normalizePhone_`, `trimLower_`, `removeDiacritics_`, `canonicalizeUrl_`, `isRealUrl_`, `extractDomainFromUrl_`). Zadne paralelni normalizacni funkce.
- **Null vs empty policy:** `phone`, `email`, `website_url` jsou vzdy string (`""` pokud invalid, nikdy `null`). Ostatni optional pole (`ico`, `contact_name`, `district`, `area`, `segment`, `service_type`, `pain_point`, `rating`, `reviews_count`) zustavaji `null`. `has_website` se vzdy dopocitava z `website_url` jako `"yes"`/`"no"`.
- **Reject rules:** `INVALID_PAYLOAD_JSON`, `MISSING_BUSINESS_NAME`, `MISSING_CITY`, `NO_CONTACT_CHANNELS`, `NORMALIZATION_FAILED`, `LEADS_WRITE_FAILED`.
- **Numeric rules:** `rating` valid iff [0, 5] (no clamp, out-of-range -> null). `reviews_count` via `parseInt(v, 10)` with leading-digit semantics, negativ -> null.
- **lead_id:** reuse format `ASW-{ts36}-{rnd4}` z `PreviewPipeline.gs:63-108`. Generator se extrahuje do sdileneho `generateLeadId_()` v `Helpers.gs`, volany jak existujicim `ensureLeadIds()` backfillem, tak novym import writerem. Immutable po LEADS insertu. Retry = novy raw radek s novym raw_import_id -> novy lead_id.
- **Source metadata:** 6 novych sloupcu append-only na konec `EXTENSION_COLUMNS`: `source_job_id`, `source_portal`, `source_url`, `source_raw_import_id`, `source_scraped_at`, `source_imported_at`. Vsechny s `source_` prefixem.
- **LEADS schema governance:** `LEGACY_COL` pozice 1-20 nedotceno, `validateLegacyColHeaders_()` guard projde beze zmeny. `setupPreviewExtension()` automaticky doplni nove sloupce.
- **Zavislosti:** A-01 (source_job_id FK), A-02 (_raw_import row shape).

## Known Limits

- **Zadna implementace normalizatoru.** A-03 je kontraktovy task. Implementace (`normalizeRawImportRow_()` v `apps-script/` nebo ekvivalent) je samostatny code task a bude resen v code PR navazujicim na tento.
- **Rating scale per portal** — firmy.cz a zivefirmy.cz mohou vracet ruzne formaty (`"4.5"`, `"4,5"`, moznost `"4,5/5"` s suffixem). Aktualni rule pokryva `.` a `,` decimal, ale ne `/5` suffix. Potvrdit v A-04.
- **`segment` aliasy mezi portaly** — pokud portaly pouzivaji ruzne katalogy kategorii, mapping na kanonicke segment hodnoty je zodpovednost scraperu (A-04), ne normalizatoru.
- **Fake `contact_name` tokens** — seznam (`info`, `sales`, `office`, ...) je heuristicky. Pilot ukaze, zda rozsirit nebo zrusit.
- **Decision log entry** — zvazeno, ale `docs/01-decision-list.md` je seznam OTEVRENYCH rozhodnuti cekajicich na owner input, ne audit log hotovych rozhodnuti. A-03 rozhodnuti byla ucinena v chatu, neni co otevrit -> zadny zapis do decision-list.md.
